<p><b>Regula</b> is a free, fully offline Catholic moral and doctrinal reference for Android. Browse clear answers to everyday moral questions, see how each teaching is classified, and read paraphrased citations from magisterial sources — with no internet connection, no analytics, and no tracking.</p>

<p><b>Features</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Eight topic categories — sacraments, sexual ethics, bioethics, daily moral life, social teaching, prayer, ecclesiology, and death &amp; afterlife</li>
<li>Concise Q&amp;A entries with classification badges: dogma, doctrine, discipline, or theological opinion</li>
<li>Citations from the Catechism, canon law, magisterial documents, Aquinas, and Scripture</li>
<li>Full-text search across questions and answers</li>
<li>Dark, readable Material 3 interface</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Privacy by design</b></p>
<p>Regula requests no network permission. All content is bundled locally. There are no proprietary SDKs, no Firebase, and no Google Play Services dependency.</p>
